Think I was bored of studying and decided it was time to procrastinate a little bit. I always wanted to customise my lockscreen (the omarchy default not inspiring me much), and with a recently purchased claude subscription for the sake of summarising some slides I started to play with Claude code. The lockscreen turned out a huge success, the loyal rodeheads out there gave their praise and so I moved onto a custom wallpaper/widget. I thus needed a better way of sharing my creations, for lack of a better word, and so decided to make a website. I had purchased the eliasro.de domain name a couple months prior and so it was relatively easy, and free, to whip together a website over the course of a day. Now that this blog post is online and operational, I will take my leave for a couple of days while I work on my studies, holidays, and more corporate parts of my online image (feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn). Please find attached photos of previous work, which inspired this website.
